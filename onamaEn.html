ABOUT US

Kovačević transport Ltd. was founded in 1996 in Futog, Serbia. Ever since it was founded, the company strived to provide top-quality logistics and transport services internationally. We always aspire to justify the trust of our existing clients through high-quality logistics and transport services and use this reputation to gain new clients.

We try to always maintain a high level of logistics and transport services which is why our core values are quality and safety. Our trucks have EURO 5 and EURO 6 certified engines. All our vehicles have CMR-insurance and TIR carnets, and they are all equipped with GPS trackers.
